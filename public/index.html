Instructions

Controles

Use las flechas de su teclado o [W,A,S,D] para jugar en el juego.

Para pausar / resumir el juego presione [SPACIO] o [ESC] o solo haga clic en el area del juego.

Controles

Use gestos de deslizamiento para jugar en el juego.

Alternativamente use los Botones de Flechas para jugar en el juego.

Para pausar / resumir el juego, toque el area de juego una vez.

Clouds

Clouds are creatures that hunt you and will kill you if they catch you.

Every cloud has its own strategy to chase you.

Inky

Inky will stay in the cloud house until you has eaten at least 30 pills. His home is the bottom right corner.

Blinky

Blinky is the most agressive of the 4 clouds. He will start chasing you right away, and aim directly at you. His home is the upper right corner.

Pinky

Pinky will start chasing you right away, he will always aim 4 fields ahead and 4 fields left of you. His home is the upper left corner.

Clyde

Clyde will stay in the ghost house until you has eaten at least 2/3 of all pills. His home is the bottom left corner.

Cloud moods

The clouds have two different moods that change the way they act during the game.

Scatter mood

This is the default mood. When clouds are in scatter mood, they will just go to their home corner and stay there.

Chase mood

After a certain time the clouds change their mood and want to go chasing you. This is indicated through the walls turning red.

Items

Pills

The goal of every level is, to eat all the white pills without getting catched by the clouds. One pill results in 10 points.

Powerpills

In every level there are 4 powerpills, which are a bit bigger than the regular ones. If You eats those, you will get strong enough to eat the clouds. You can see this indicated by the clouds turning blue. One powerpill results in 50 points.

Eating a cloud results in 100 points. The soul of the cloud will return to the cloud house before starting to chase you again.
